Parent Education

Reaching parents when and wherever is best for them is the key to this research-based, asset building program. The four-session program may be scheduled for individuals or groups. Program discussion includes the foundations for parenting success. Program information is also adapted to meet specific, identified needs of parents, including family budget planning.
